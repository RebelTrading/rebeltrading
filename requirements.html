Application Requirements

Rebel Trading Group is a performance-based futures trading environment. We do not accept general applications. All traders are evaluated based on real historical performance and disciplined risk behavior before being granted access to our simulation system.

To be considered, traders must demonstrate a verifiable trading history showing consistency, controlled risk management, and repeatable execution. We are not looking for isolated high-profit months or high-risk account spikes — we are looking for structured, professional trading behavior.

Minimum Requirements

• Minimum 12 months of trading history OR 300+ executed trades
• Minimum 60% win rate over full trading history
• Maximum drawdown between 10%–15% (verified via journal)
• No single month may account for more than 80% of total profits
• Evidence of consistent risk management (no blow-up patterns)

All applicants must submit a verified TradeZella journal or equivalent trading report along with a short cover letter explaining their strategy, risk approach, and trading style.

Only traders who meet these standards will be granted access to the 100K simulation evaluation environment. This process ensures that every trader entering our system has already demonstrated baseline discipline before evaluation begins.
