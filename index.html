Who We Are

Rebel Trading Group is a private futures trading firm built around one principle: consistency over hype.

We do not operate like traditional prop firms. There are no mass signups, no monthly fees, and no system designed for failure. We recruit and develop a small number of traders who already demonstrate edge and discipline.

Our model is simple — identify strong traders, refine execution, and allocate capital only when probability is in our favor.

Applications & Selection

We only accept traders who demonstrate real edge. This is not open enrollment.

Phase 1 Requirements:

• Minimum 12 months trading history OR 300+ trades
• Minimum 60% win rate
• Max drawdown: 10–15%
• No single month > 80% of total profits
• Consistent, controlled risk behavior

Only traders who meet these standards are offered access to our evaluation environment.

Accepted Platforms

All Rebel Trading evaluations are conducted through approved simulation platforms. These environments are used to track performance, enforce discipline, and ensure consistency across all traders.

MetaTrader 5 (MT5)

We use MetaTrader 5 for structured futures simulation environments. Traders are provided access to a controlled demo account where performance is monitored in real time.

• Desktop & Web supported
• Real-time simulated execution
• Full trade history visibility

TradingView

TradingView is used for chart execution and visual trade tracking within our evaluation system. Traders execute trades using a provided simulation login.

• Web-based execution
• Instant chart access
• Ideal for intraday futures analysis

All trader performance is tracked internally regardless of platform. Our focus is consistency, risk control, and execution quality — not the platform itself.
