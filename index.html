Joining Rebel Trading

Rebel Trading is a performance-based trading system built for disciplined stock traders. There are no subscriptions, no courses, and no application process.

Traders simply select an evaluation package and begin trading in a structured simulated environment with defined risk parameters.

Our system is built on a two-stage progression model:

Stage 1 — Evaluation Phase: Traders must reach the profit target while respecting strict risk limits. Failure occurs only if drawdown thresholds are breached. There are no artificial time constraints or trailing/EOD drawdown mechanics.

Stage 2 — Funded Sim Phase: Traders who pass the evaluation are promoted into a funded simulation account where they trade with scaled capital under live market conditions.

In the funded sim phase, traders are continuously measured on key performance metrics including: consistent profitability, drawdown control, risk-to-reward efficiency, and position sizing discipline.

Traders who maintain strong performance metrics over time in the funded sim phase may be invited to join Rebel Trading as live account traders, managing real capital with an 80/20 profit split structure.

Rebel Trading is designed as a performance pipeline: only traders who demonstrate consistency and risk control across both evaluation and funded simulation phases are considered for live capital allocation.
