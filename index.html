Who is Rebel Trading?

Rebel Trading Group was founded by Teddy Teske in 2026 after more than 20 years of trading stocks, options, and intraday scalping systems. Built from real market execution experience, the firm focuses on identifying disciplined traders and refining execution under pressure.

Our approach is simple: remove noise, enforce structure, and evaluate traders based on consistency—not hype or single trades.

Applications & Recruitment

Rebel Trading accepts traders through two entry paths:

1. Applications: Traders may submit a TradeZella journal or verified trade history along with a short cover letter.

2. Recruitment: Traders identified through performance or referrals may be invited directly into evaluation.

All applicants must demonstrate real trading activity and risk behavior before being considered for evaluation access.

100K Sim Evaluation (Unified Account)

All approved traders are placed into a single structured simulation environment: TradingView “Sim Trader 01” — $100,000 Paper Account

Traders are given access to trade freely across NASDAQ equities, SPY, and all major US-listed stocks. There are no restricted symbols.

Profit Target

Risk Rules

• Static drawdown: 3% ($3,000 max loss)

Consistency Requirement

Traders who meet all requirements pass into the funded simulation stage, where performance is tracked under scaled capital conditions and longer-term evaluation metrics.
