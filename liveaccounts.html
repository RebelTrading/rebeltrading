How Live Funding Works

Live accounts are NOT entry-level products. They are reserved exclusively for traders who successfully complete our full selection pipeline:

• 60%+ win rate across 300+ trades (initial screening)
• Approval into the 100K evaluation environment
• Completion of the 90-day internal training program

Only traders who demonstrate consistency, discipline, and controlled risk are eligible for live capital allocation.

There is no cost to access live accounts. Capital is assigned internally after qualification.
