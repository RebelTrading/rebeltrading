What is the Membership?

The Rebel Trading Membership is an elite access program designed for serious traders who want real-time exposure to a professional trading environment. For just $50/month, members gain direct access to our live trading ecosystem, tools, and mentorship.

Live Trade Desk Access

Every morning from 4:00 AM MST to 7:00 AM MST, members can join our live trade desk and:

  • Watch real-time trade executions
  • Follow professional decision-making in live markets
  • Access custom stock & futures screeners
  • View live news flow and catalysts
  • Engage in live chat discussions
  • Observe structured chart breakdowns
